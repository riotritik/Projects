Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is brain tumor detection?

    Brain tumor detection is the process of identifying the presence of tumors in the brain. It is a critical process that can help in the early diagnosis and treatment of brain cancer.

  • How is brain tumor detection performed?

    Brain tumor detection can be performed using a variety of imaging tests, such as magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) and computed tomography (CT) scans.

  • What are the symptoms of brain tumors?

    The symptoms of brain tumors can vary depending on their location and size, but may include headaches, seizures, vision changes, and difficulty with balance and coordination.
